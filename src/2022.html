Seulmi kang
NOTATION CREATIVE CONSULTING
BOUBA AND KIKI – Designing interaction with audio and visual cues
We immediately recognize that a message has arrived by hearing a simple jingle from a mobile phone. Sound, together with visuals, plays a crucial role in delivering meanings and emotions in user interfaces. How does everyday interaction with products and services work, and how does it influence shaping our behavior? We will explore our association with sound and visuals in this session. With playful activities, we will experience the role of sound in product design and its principle.
16:00
Lorenzo monti
MESSIEURS.CH
An attempt to untangle art & design
Gather around for an intimate session with director and video editor, Lorenzo Monti, to question art and design.
Bring fresh air into the loop and recognise when to be in the box or out of the box, quite literally play with these boxes, and discover oneself along the process.
16:00
ROLAND Vollenweider
v0ll
Modular synths & patterns with step sequencers
The Zurich based artist v0II takes you into a world full of repetitive patterns and surprising textures. This session will be in german.
16:00
Jonas Wyssen
INTERDISCIPLINARY DESIGNER / ARTIST
Looking AI in the eyes – How to get creative with artificial intelligence
In this session, Jonas shows his work «Da Vinci Robots - machines drawn by the machine. The aim is to open a dialogue about artificial intelligence in art and design. After a look behind the curtain, we will dive into the creative process using machine learning models like DALLE2 & Midjourney and better understand which role creatives will play in this unknown field.
